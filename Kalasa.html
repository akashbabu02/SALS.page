SALS Education Campus Drive Achievements at Kalasalingam College of Engineering and Technology

We are thrilled to announce the outstanding success of the campus recruitment drive conducted by SALS Education at Rohini College of Engineering and Technology. This event marked a significant milestone in our continued efforts to bridge the gap between talented graduates and their dream careers. Here’s a snapshot of our collective achievements:

  • Exceptional Placement Record: The campus drive saw an impressive participation rate from our final year students, with a remarkable 85% of the attendees receiving job offers. This achievement underscores the effectiveness of our rigorous academic programs and the dedication of our students.
  • Diverse Opportunities: SALS Education brought forth a wide array of opportunities across various roles in education technology, content development, marketing, and operations. This diversity not only catered to the varied interests and skills of our students but also highlighted the multidisciplinary excellence cultivated at Rohini College.
  • Competitive Salary Packages: The event set a new benchmark with some of the highest salary packages offered in our campus drive history. The average salary package offered was 20% higher than the previous year, with the top offer reaching an impressive figure that underscores the value of our students in the job market.
  • Skill-Based Hiring: Reflecting a modern approach to recruitment, SALS Education focused on skill and competency-based hiring rather than solely on academic performance. This methodology aligned well with our pedagogical approach, emphasizing practical skills, critical thinking, and adaptability.
  • Industry-Academia Collaboration: The drive facilitated an enhanced partnership between Kalasalingam College and SALS Education, paving the way for future collaborations, including internships, live projects, and guest lectures. This synergy is aimed at keeping our curriculum relevant and industry-focused, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for their professional journeys.
  • Student Development: Leading up to the drive, our students participated in several workshops and seminars conducted by SALS Education, focusing on resume building, interview preparation, and soft skills enhancement. These initiatives significantly boosted their confidence and preparedness for the recruitment process.
  • 7. Feedback and Insights: Post-event feedback sessions with SALS Education representatives provided valuable insights into industry expectations and trends. This feedback will be instrumental in further refining our courses and training programs.
Conclution

The successful campus drive by SALS Education at Kalasalingam College of Engineering and Technology stands as a testament to the potential and readiness of our students to excel in the competitive job market. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to SALS Education for their partnership and to our students, faculty, and the placement cell for making this event a resounding success. We look forward to building on these achievements and to even more successful collaborations in the future.
